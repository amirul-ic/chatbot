UNIFI SPEED UPGRADE 2023

At Unifi, we strive to give the best digital experience and value to our customers. We are automatically upgrading the Internet speeds for our Unifi Home and Unifi Biz customers, with no extra charge. Enjoy bigger bandwidth with our worry-free connectivity on more devices to stream, game and download for work and play.

This free speed upgrade will be rolled out to over 3 Million Unifi customers, driving home our commitment to deliver higher speeds at lower prices to all. The transition is anticipated to take place in phases between now until December 2023.

Thank you, we are honoured to be part of your digital world! 

*For eligible Unifi Home and Unifi Biz customers, subject to fibre coverage and infrastructure availability. Other T&Cs apply.
Faster Speed

Get upgraded up to 3x from your current speed

No changes to your Unifi bill

You will continue to receive your bill with the usual amount

Step 3

Enjoy higher speed

All eligible customers will be upgraded by default, transition to take place from now until December 2023. Enjoy faster internet at home!

WHAT YOU NEED TO KNOW

Who is eligible for the Speed Upgrade?

Selected Unifi Home and Unifi Biz customers will enjoy the speed upgrade, with the same price subscribed plan subjected to:-

  • Unifi fibre coverage and availability
  • Existing network infrastructure

When will I enjoy the higher speed?

Eligible customers will be upgraded in phases, from October 2023 to December 2023.

How do I know if my plan has been upgraded?

You may perform a speed test to your Unifi Home/ Unifi Biz internet connection. 

To test your speed, please proceed to speed test.
